What is Mendix?

Mendix is a leading Low-Code/No-Code application development platform that empowers businesses to rapidly design, build, and deploy applications with minimal coding effort. By combining visual development tools, drag-and-drop interfaces, and pre-built templates, Mendix streamlines the development process, making it accessible to both technical and non-technical users.
